About

I am a graphic designer and a letterer based in New England. I also work as a web designer/developer for Leverett Media in Boston. Fun fact about me: I like to hoard type-related things like patches, books, old posters, magazines, labels, etc. I’m a typomaniac; let’s leave it at that.

Many of the skills I have developed were thanks to self-initiated side-projects and client work. Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic Web Design at Southern New Hampshire University while working as a web designer.
